Our Mission

To support the expansion of the gospel ministry by providing biblically based financial services to church ministers, members, and the Christian community in Ethiopia, fostering a culture of savings, providing responsible microfinance services, and bringing about sustainable economic growth by transforming the lives of its members.

Detailed Objectives

1
To encourage the Christian community to save and work, to create a sustainable financial institution and provide banking services to its members, to increase their income and reduce poverty,
2
To encourage and support women and youth who need special assistance, as well as the disabled, to engage in income-generating activities and increase their income,
3
To contribute to the development plans set by our country's government,
4
To increase their participation in the cooperative by providing continuous training and education to members, management, employees and the community,
5
To expand the accessibility of the service by providing fast and efficient services to members using modern technologies, to strengthen the social power of the church and support the spread of the gospel,
6
To benefit its members from profits by engaging in viable business sectors

Membership is open to:

  • Full-time ministers and support workers; adult church members (14+)
  • Women, youth and low-income families
  • Small church-based savings associations
  • Church landowners and landowners
  • Brotherhoods (sisterhoods)
  • Church itself (local, regional, district)
  • Open to all denominations within the church network
